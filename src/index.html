Our Services

Your Office Team — Without the Overhead

We take care of the paperwork so you can stay focused on what you do best — running jobs and building your business. Whether you're a solo contractor wearing multiple hats or managing a growing team, we’re here to lighten the load. From the moment a lead comes in to the final invoice and payroll, we handle the full admin cycle behind the scenes. Our job is to make sure your back office runs smoothly, so you can stay on-site, on schedule, and in control.

  • Contracts

    Our contracts are tailored to your work and written to protect you. Whether it’s a simple one-page agreement or a multi-phase job with change orders, we’ll make sure your terms are clear and legally sound — no more grey areas.

  • Quotes, Estimates & Bids

    We handle everything from quick quotes to full bid packages — all tailored to your trade and client. Whether you’re sending a simple estimate or responding to a competitive tender, we’ll prepare clean, professional documents that reflect your pricing, scope, and branding. We’ll make sure you stand out — and submit on time.

  • Invoicing & Payroll

    We take care of the money going in and out. From sending invoices and tracking payments to organizing payroll and managing your crew’s pay schedules, we’ll keep your finances moving and your team paid — without the stress or confusion.

  • Scheduling & Job Tracking

    Avoid missed jobs and double-booking with clear schedules and job trackers built around how you work. We’ll help you plan ahead, stay on track, and manage multiple projects without the chaos.
